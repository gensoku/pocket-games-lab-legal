4. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

4.1 Right of Access

You can request a copy of all your personal data.

4.2 Right to Rectification

You can modify your information directly in the app (Profile > Settings).

4.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request complete deletion of your data:

  • In-app: Settings > Account > Delete my account
  • By email: Contact us at [YOUR EMAIL] with your UID or email

Timeline: Maximum 30 days (usually within 7 days)

Effect: Irreversible deletion of all your personal and progress data

4.4 Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a structured format (JSON) to transfer it elsewhere.

4.5 Right to Object and Restriction

You can:

  • Disable Firebase Analytics in settings
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Purchase ad removal (remove_ads IAP)

4.6 Right to Complaint

If you believe your rights are not being respected, you can lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g., ICO in UK, CNIL in France).

5. Data Security

5.1 Technical Measures

  • Encryption: HTTPS/TLS for all communications
  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication with bcrypt password hashing
  • Firestore Rules: Strict per-user access restrictions
  • Server validation: Cloud Functions to validate IAP purchases
  • Local storage: Sensitive data encrypted on device

5.2 Limitations (Solo Developer)

As an independent developer, I commit to doing my best to protect your data, but I cannot guarantee absolute security (no system is 100% secure). In case of a data breach, you will be notified promptly in accordance with GDPR.

7. Children and Minors

Pocket Games Lab is designed to be suitable for all ages (PEGI 3 / ESRB Everyone).

  • Under 13 years (COPPA): Requires parental consent. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental agreement.
  • 13-16 years (GDPR): May vary by country. Parental consent recommended.
  • Parental control: Parents can request deletion of their child's data by contacting us.

8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The app uses local storage technologies:

  • PlayerPrefs (Unity): User preferences, settings
  • Firebase Local Persistence: Offline cache for improved performance
  • Advertising ID: Used by Unity Ads (can be reset in Android settings)

These are not traditional "web cookies" but function similarly.

9. Offline Mode

The app works offline:

  • Automatic synchronization upon reconnection
  • No analytics data sent offline
