This Privacy Policy explains how PocketGamesLab ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Anonymous authentication ID (Firebase Authentication)
  • Optional email and password if you choose to link an account
  • Email verification status

1.2 Gameplay & Profile Data

  • Progression data (levels, achievements, unlocked modes)
  • Virtual currency (gems) balance and spending
  • Game statistics (scores, time played, difficulty)
  • Profile customization (avatar, background, border, UI skin)
  • Display name (chosen by the user, non-unique)

1.3 Analytics Data

We collect non-sensitive analytics data using Firebase Analytics, such as:

  • Game sessions started or ended
  • Game modes played
  • Difficulty selected
  • Virtual currency spending events
  • Achievements unlocked
  • UI skin changes
  • Language changes
  • Error and critical error events

Important: No personally identifiable information is included in analytics events.

1.4 Advertising Data

The App displays advertisements:

  • Interstitial ads between game modes
  • Rewarded ads for temporary unlocks

Advertising partners may collect limited data required to serve ads, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

2. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases via Google Play Billing, including:

  • Consumable virtual currency packs
  • Non-consumable items (ad removal, UI skins)

Important: All purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not store payment or billing information.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's features
  • Save game progress and user preferences
  • Enable purchases and virtual content
  • Improve gameplay experience
  • Monitor app stability and errors

4. Data Storage and Security

  • User data is stored securely using Firebase Firestore
  • Access to user data is restricted to the user and authorized services only
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties, except:

  • Firebase services (authentication, analytics, storage)
  • Advertising providers required to display ads
  • Google Play services for purchases

6. Your Rights (GDPR & International Users)

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us at Genso.dev@gmail.com
  • Modify your profile information within the app
  • Disable analytics collection in Settings
  • Request deletion of your data

To request a copy of your personal data, please send an email to Genso.dev@gmail.com with your User ID (found in Profile > Account). We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Deletion

7.1 Account Data

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • Go to Profile > Account > Delete Account
  • All gameplay data, progress, achievements, and profile information will be immediately deleted
  • This action cannot be undone

7.2 Analytics Data

Analytics collection can be controlled independently from your account:

  • Go to Settings > Other to disable future analytics collection
  • Previously collected analytics data is anonymous and not linked to your personal account
  • Disabling analytics does not affect your gameplay or account
